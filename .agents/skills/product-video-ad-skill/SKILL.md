# Product Video Ad Skill

## Purpose

Use this Skill when an AI agent needs to turn a product into short ad video concepts.

Built for [AIADS.IM](https://aiads.im), an AI Ads Generator for UGC video ads and product video ads.

## Inputs

Ask for or infer:

- product name
- product category
- product URL
- product image description
- main selling point
- target customer
- platform
- duration
- offer
- brand voice
- proof point

## Output

Return:

1. product brief
2. target audience
3. 5 ad angles
4. 10 hooks
5. 3 product video scripts
6. scene-by-scene storyboard
7. captions
8. CTA variants
9. visual notes for the video generator

## Product video structures

Use these structures:

- Problem -> product reveal -> benefit -> proof -> CTA
- Product demo -> feature -> use case -> CTA
- Before -> after -> product reason -> CTA
- Unboxing -> close-up -> result -> CTA
- Comparison -> why this product -> CTA

## Rules

- Do not invent product claims.
- Do not invent reviews.
- Do not claim guaranteed results.
- Keep shots simple and feasible for AI video generation.
- Prefer visual proof over abstract claims.

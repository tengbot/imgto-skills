# Product Video Template

Use this template to build product video ad briefs.

Main site: https://aiads.im

## Brief

```yaml
product:
category:
audience:
main_benefit:
visual_proof:
platform:
duration:
offer:
cta:
```

## Product video structure

```text
Hook
Product reveal
Demo moment
Benefit explanation
Proof or reason to believe
CTA
```

## Output format

```yaml
ad_angle:
hook:
script:
storyboard:
  - scene:
    duration:
    visual:
    on_screen_text:
    voiceover:
    product_focus:
cta_options:
caption_options:
video_generation_notes:
```

## Shot ideas

- close-up product reveal
- product in hand
- problem scene
- product solving the problem
- before and after
- product detail macro shot
- package shot
- offer and CTA end card

## Rules

- Keep product visible.
- Make the first shot strong.
- Keep claims tied to supplied facts.
- Avoid fake reviews or fake proof.

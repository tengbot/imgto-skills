## Minimum brief

If the user provides almost nothing, ask for these fields first:

- product name
- product category
- target customer
- main benefit
- platform
- desired ad style

## Quality rules

- Do not invent proof.
- Do not invent reviews.
- Do not promise guaranteed outcomes.
- Keep claims aligned with the supplied product brief.
- Ask for missing facts when claims require evidence.
